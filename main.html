I am currently working as a NLP Research Associate at the Laboratory for Computational Social Systems (LCS2), an inter institute lab between IIT Delhi and IIIT-Delhi , where I am advised by Prof. Tanmoy Chakraborty. As my first project, I explored hate speech in electoral politics in India followed by an industry collaboration with RAxter division of Crimson Interactive to help build their inline citation classification system to support research scholars in various stages of their research. Currently, I am extending my knowledge by working in the Hyperbolic Space on Multimodal Information Retrieval and Feed Item Recommendation in collaboration with SAMSUNG Research where I am forming a taxonomy for image, speech, and text data. My primary research interests lie in:

- Computational Social Science
- Machine/Speech Translation
- AI in Law
- Financial NLP
- NLP Applications
